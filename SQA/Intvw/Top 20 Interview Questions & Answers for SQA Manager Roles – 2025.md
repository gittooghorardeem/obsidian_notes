
> [**Top 20 Interview Questions 2025**](https://www.cvowl.com/blog/sqa-manager-interview-questions-answers)


1. **What inspired you to pursue a career in SQA management?**
2. **What do you think are the most important qualities of a successful SQA manager?**
3. **How do you prioritize and manage your tasks as an SQA manager?**
4. **How do you stay updated on the latest advancements and best practices in software quality assurance?**
5. **Can you walk us through your experience in leading a team of SQA professionals?**
6. **What metrics do you use to measure the effectiveness of your software quality assurance process?**
7. **Can you tell us about a time when you encountered a major software bug and how you solved it?**
8. **How do you ensure that software testing is performed efficiently and thoroughly?**
9. **Have you implemented any new processes or tools to improve the software quality assurance process in your current role?**
10. **How do you handle conflicts or disagreements within your team?**
11. **Can you tell us about a time when you had to handle a difficult stakeholder in a software quality assurance project?**
12. **Describe a situation where you had to make a difficult decision as an SQA manager.**
13. **How do you handle high-pressure situations or tight deadlines in an SQA project?**
14. **Tell us about a time when you had to resolve a conflict between a team member and a developer during a software development project.**
15. **How do you ensure that your team is aligned with the company's values and culture?**
16. **If you notice that a software product is facing many quality issues, what steps would you take to address the situation?**
17. **What would you do if you encounter a compatibility issue between the software and a specific device during testing?**
18. **How do you handle a situation when the requirements for a project change midway through the development process?**
19. **What steps would you take if you notice that the software development team is not adhering to the defined quality standards?**
20. **How do you handle a software defect that is discovered in the production environment?**
